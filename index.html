Meet Shelly Hornback

Owner & Principal

With over 20 years of strategic communications experience, Shelly brings proven expertise from executive leadership in K-12 education and grassroots advocacy. As former Director of Community Relations for an award-winning 12,000-student district, she managed multi-million dollar campaigns, crisis communications, and stakeholder engagement across diverse communities.

Her track record includes securing millions in voter-approved funding, managing media relationships that generated substantial earned media value, and leading successful communications during high-stakes challenges like COVID response and major organizational changes. Shelly's expertise spans media relations, digital campaigns, partnership development, and crisis management.

A longtime Arizona resident, Shelly brings her proven strategic communications expertise to Phoenix-area businesses, non-profits, and organizations through Hornback Communications. Her deep community knowledge and commitment to authentic relationship-building help clients navigate complex challenges and achieve meaningful results.
