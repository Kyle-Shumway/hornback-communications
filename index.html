About Us

Hornback Communications is a boutique public relations firm based in Phoenix, Arizona. We help our clients (school districts, businesses, nonprofits, and municipalities) foster enduring community connections. Experience, honesty and a passion for servant leadership fuel our commitment to attentive service and effective outcomes.

Shelly Smith Hornback

Owner & Principal

With a robust community relations background, Shelly is a trusted community leader and seasoned communications professional with a flair for making connections and inspiring engagement. We build on Shelly's quarter century of communications experience to help clients effectively nurture relationships.

We know how to engage target audiences because of Shelly's robust professional experiences as an elected governing board member and school district administrator, nonprofit board member, coalition builder and political action committee chairperson.

Shelly serves on the boards of Common Sense Media (AZ), School Connect and Ignite Yoga Foundation. She is a member of Charter 100, a prestigious network organization for Arizona women leaders of outstanding accomplishments in diverse fields. As chair of a tri-district political action committee, Shelly led nearly two dozen winning campaigns, ensuring hundreds of millions of dollars for teaching positions and new school construction.

Our Services

Communications

We help our clients grow and improve by developing strategic communications plans, creating and pitching news stories, and writing compelling content. Services include strategic communications planning, stakeholder engagement, media relations, and thought leadership.

Community Relations

We help our clients attract and maintain quality partners, oftentimes facilitating the introduction, to leverage resources and deepen impact. Connecting quality folks to do good work together is one of our favorite things! Services include strategic community partnerships and corporate giving strategy & branding.

Training

A variety of professional development opportunities help our clients build capacity. Training areas include community engagement, public speaking, public relations, media relations, thought leadership, customer experience audits & training, ambassador programs, and volunteer services.
